Rafting Site Plan

Kim Brown

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of establishing an online presence for the rafting business is to enhance visibility and reach a wider audience by providing a centralized platform for potential customers to learn about the company's offerings, explore trip options, and easily make reservations. The website aims to showcase the thrilling experience of water rafting, highlight the business's unique selling points, and foster engagement with adventure enthusiasts seeking exciting outdoor activities.

Audience

The target audience for the water rafting website includes adventurous outdoor enthusiasts seeking thrilling experiences. This encompasses a diverse group, including young adults in their mid-20s looking for group adventures, families with children aged 8 to 12 seeking a memorable vacation activity, and solo travelers aged 30 seeking unique and challenging experiences.

Scenario Questions and Answers

Question 1:

Question: Imagine a group of friends in their mid-20s planning an adventurous getaway. What would attract them to our water rafting services?

Answer: Our water rafting services cater to thrill-seekers seeking an adrenaline-packed experience. With challenging rapids, stunning river landscapes, and expert guides, we provide the perfect adventure for young adults looking to create unforgettable memories with friends.

Question 2:

Question: A family with children aged 8 to 12 is looking for a memorable outdoor activity during their vacation. How does our website appeal to family-oriented customers?

Answer: Our website emphasizes family-friendly rafting trips with a focus on safety and excitement suitable for children. Informative content on trip suitability for different age groups, safety measures, and engaging visuals showcasing families enjoying our rafting adventures will address the needs of family-oriented customers.

Question 3:

Question: An avid solo traveler, aged 30, is seeking a unique and challenging experience. How does our website cater to solo adventurers?

Answer: Solo adventurers can find detailed information on our website about specially designed solo-friendly trips. We address the needs of solo travelers by providing information on the camaraderie experienced during group trips, safety measures in place, and opportunities to connect with like-minded individuals for an exciting solo journey.
